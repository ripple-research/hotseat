// Your mission briefing
The
Hot
Seat.
Today, you are on the other side.

You have been hired by a group that wants to stop the Living Landscapes Act. Your job is to build a message that turns people against it.

You have 20 minutes. You have a target audience. And you have a simple formula.

Let's see how far you get.

// STEP A
Read the background
Learn about Veritania and the Living Landscapes Act before you begin.
// STEP B
Study the example
See a completed message before you build your own. Takes 2 minutes.
// STEP C
Take the Hot Seat
Build your opposition message step by step. One person types, everyone decides.

One person should control this screen. Agree who that is before you begin. Only one person typing at a time will keep your group's answers consistent.

Once you confirm each choice, you cannot go back and change it. Make sure your group agrees before you click.
